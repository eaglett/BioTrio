BioTrio is the leading cinema chain in Denmark,
with a total of 1 cinemas and approximately 10 million guests a year.

All auditoriums are equipped with the best and most recent digital technology and we make a dedicated effort to develop our cinemas and build new ones. Our main objective is to ensure very happy cinema goers, but cinemas are about much more than showing movies. A number of the auditoriums can be transformed into stages and concert halls. In cooperation with The Metropolitan Opera, we broadcast operas directly from New York, and the cinemas regularly hosts all kinds of special events, and Imperial in Copenhagen, the largest single auditorium in Northern Europe, is extensively used for gala premieres and award shows.

How to find us?

We are located very centrally, in the "Indre By" of Copenhagen. You can access us by taking metro, bus or bike. We also offer 3 hours of free parking if you make a purchase bigger than 500kr.

We value your privacy. All of our procedures are GDPR friendly. For more information contact us on biotrio@cinema.dk
